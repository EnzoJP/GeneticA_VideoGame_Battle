-> Ataques y probabilidades

- Strike Attack: Normal attack using the Strike attribute. (Si SP=0)
- Maragidyne: Deals heavy Fire damage to all foes. (39%)  Coste: 24 SP.
- Hamaon (Light): instant kill, 1 foe (high odds). (40% chance) (25%) Coste 12 SP.
- Megidola: Deals heavy Almighty damage to all foes. (15%) Coste: 65 SP.
- Evil Touch: Instills Fear in 1 foe. (40% chance) (19%) Coste: 5 SP.
- Ghastly Wail: Instantly kills all foes who are fearful. (Si miembros de la party (al menos uno) tienen el estado de fear o miedo) Coste: 15 SP.
- Fear Boost: Increases odds of instilling Fear by 25%. PASIVA
